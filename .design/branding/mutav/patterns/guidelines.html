06 · Visual Elements

Architecture as argument.

In MUTAV, photography is not decoration — it is evidence. Every scene carries the same logic as the product: visible structure, honest material, no element without function. The photo is always black and white. Amber enters as a solid geometric shape, never as a filter or direct colorization. The subject is architecture: concrete, modular façades, exposed structure. The scale of the building communicates the weight of the guarantee.

Subject

Urban and residential architecture

Brutalist buildings, exposed concrete, modular façades, repetitive structure. No people as main subject.

Angle

Worm's eye dominant

Camera pointed upward from below. Amplifies scale and weight. Close-up on façade texture also valid.

Treatment

Black and white, full desaturation

No direct colorization of the photo. Subtle grain/noise applied. No blur, no bokeh — everything in focus.

Accent color

MUTAV amber as a separate shape

Color enters over the photo as an independent graphic element: circle, rectangle, line, or outline. Never a filter.

Typography

Geist Bold at oversized scale

Text occupies physical area, doesn't just communicate. May bleed off-frame. Always three-layer hierarchy: Geist / Inter / Mono.

Composition

Photo covers 60–80% of total area

Accent shape creates central tension. Text anchors at edges (top left + base). Never centered and symmetric.

Composition system

Three levels of visual complexity, from most contained to most tense. Each level defines how many photos, which accent shape, and how much text the composition supports.

Visual constraints

Each rule has a direct opposite. The examples below show what the system accepts and what invalidates the composition.

✓ Correct — desaturated photo

Concrete.
Verified.

Photo always in black and white. The accent color is the only warm point — occupies less than 5% of the area.

✗ Forbidden — colorized photo
Concrete.
Verified.

Warmth, colorization, or amber filter applied to the photo destroys the separation between evidence (photo) and accent (color). The photo is material, not decoration.

✓ Correct — geometric shape over sharp photo

247
ativos

The accent color enters as a separate solid shape, not as an effect over the photo. Photo maintains full sharpness.

✗ Forbidden — blur / glassmorphism over photo

247 ativos

backdrop-filter: blur() and glassmorphism are forbidden across the entire MUTAV system. No element uses blur as a visual treatment.

✓ Correct — worm's eye angle, scale evident

ASCENDING ANGLE · FAÇADE

Camera pointed upward from below. The façade dominates the frame and communicates weight, scale, and permanence — attributes of the guarantee.

✗ Avoid — neutral angle, small subject

NEUTRAL ANGLE · DISTANT SUBJECT

Eye-level angle with small building in frame reduces impact and scale. The subject must dominate the frame — not be contextualized by it.

07 · Constraints

Never / Always

Never — forbidden across all fronts

  • Gradients as surface fill
  • Drop shadows (box-shadow on any element)
  • Border-radius other than 0px (cards, buttons, inputs, badges, modals)
  • Glass / backdrop-filter: blur()
  • #22C55E ANSI green on any front
  • Amber-colored icons
  • Photography inside the Investor Dashboard product
  • Blockchain jargon in the Real Estate Dashboard
  • text-shadow / phosphor glow
  • scale() or translate on hover/active
  • #000000 pure black as background
  • Decorative orbs or bloom
  • Pill/rounded badges
  • Inter inside terminal/log panes (mono only — structured rows are a separate pattern)
  • Amber on more than 5% of screen pixels

Always — required without exception

  • border-radius: 0px on all elements
  • JetBrains Mono with font-feature-settings: "tnum" 1 on all numbers
  • Three-layer hierarchy on every screen (Geist / Inter / Mono)
  • Surface stacking for depth
  • Success/error colors semantic only, never decorative
  • #1A1A1A as text on amber button in Real Estate (5.3:1 AA)
  • Inter Semi-bold 600 with #2E8B5A on light backgrounds
  • Bold labels ≥14px with #C94040 on dark backgrounds
  • Phosphor Icons at weight="light" only
  • 1px solid borders — never 2px decorative
  • 8px grid as base spacing unit
  • Minimum 1× cap height clear space around logo
  • Transitions on: color, background-color, border-color, opacity only
  • Amber scarce: less than 5% of pixels on dark fronts
