## Content Reference

Each chunk follows the standard chunk format.

### positioning.md
- Positioning statement: For {audience} who {need}, {brand} is the {category} that {benefit} because {reason}
- 2-axis positioning map with competitors plotted
- White space analysis

### archetype.md
- Primary archetype + secondary influence
- Rationale — connects to personas and competitive gaps
- Shadow traits to avoid
- Communication style — how this archetype speaks
- Visual tendencies — how this archetype looks

### brand-platform.md
- **Purpose (Why)** — reason the brand exists beyond profit
- **Vision** — the future state the brand works toward
- **Mission** — how the brand delivers on its purpose
- **Values** — behavioral values (actionable, not aspirational platitudes)
- **Promise** — the commitment to the audience

### voice-and-tone.md
- **Voice attributes** — 3-5 attributes with means/doesn't mean/examples
- **Tone spectrum** — 3-5 scales with default position + context shifts
- **Do/Don't chart** — real writing examples per attribute
- **Style rules** — contractions, emoji, exclamation marks, grammar choices
- **Nomenclature** — naming conventions for products, features, tiers

### messaging.md
- **Core message** — the one thing the brand always communicates
- **Supporting messages** — 3 messages with proof points
- **Elevator pitch** — 30-second version
- **Tagline directions** — 2-3 options with rationale
- **Audience mapping** — how messaging adapts per persona from BRIEF.md
