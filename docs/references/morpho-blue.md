## Summary

*[To be populated]* — Morpho Blue is a minimal lending primitive — one-market-per-pair, immutable, no admin keys post-deployment. The minimalism is intentional: a small audit surface that downstream protocols compose with.

## Relevance to MGV

*[To be populated]* — Morpho Blue's minimalism is the philosophical reference point for MGV's payment-system-split decision (§13a). The argument: a small, immutable, auditable vault primitive + a separate (off-chain or contract) layer for policy, partner mgmt, fee splitting, etc. Morpho's MetaMorpho vaults layer policy on top of the minimal primitive — the analog for MGV would be the payment system layer on `mutav-app`.
